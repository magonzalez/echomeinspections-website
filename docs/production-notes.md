# Production Notes

Items accepted in the design-concept mockups that MUST be addressed when
building the production site from the chosen direction:

- **Orange CTA contrast (WCAG AA):** white text on brand orange `#F97316`
  measures ≈2.8:1 (fails AA even for large text). Affects the primary CTA
  buttons in concepts 01, 02, 05, 06, 08 and the credential badge in 06.
  Fix in production: darken text-bearing orange buttons to ≈`#C2410C`
  (white ≥4.5:1) or use dark ink text on brand orange. Kept as-is in the
  mockups so the client judges the true brand color.
- Booking, report-access, and contact forms are non-functional demos; wire
  to the chosen inspection platform (Spectora / HomeGauge / ISN) or a form
  service.
- Logo: original SVG monogram created for this project; the client's
  Shutterstock reference cannot be used without licensing.
